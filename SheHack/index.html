Team "Seekers"

Our Mission

To learn and create projects to enhance our career path and boost our confidence as Computer Science engineers by gaining new skills.

Our Vision

We believe that supporting each other will help us identify our potential and achieve our career goals. We want to be an inspiration to women in technical field.

Why we want to join SheHack

As female computer science engineering students, we always wanted to learn something new, but because of a lack of good mentoring, we could not learn anything clearly and effectively. By joining SheHack, we understand that we as a Team can build a project ourselves along with the help of a mentor, which will help us gain more confidence about our capabilities.

Our Expectations

  • Vast resources to enhance our learning
  • Constant mentoring
  • Get clear understanding on how to build good projects
  • Analyse our abilities and get review from mentor
